Non-Technical Events

Non-technical skills are those that you possess that are unrelated to your specific job. These skills, also known as soft skills, are more concerned with your personal characteristics and habits than with your technical abilities. Cultural Events

Cultural events are events designed for entertainment and enjoyment of the audience. They are events of some importance related to some branch of art, culture or values. These events aim to propagate and disseminate cultural themes. It develop the process of interaction and relationship of all the participants. At the same time, they create the necessary structure for the development of interaction and relationships. It can increase individuals' confidence and give them the opportunity of effecting their personal life and development.
