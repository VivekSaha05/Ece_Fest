TECHNICAL EVENTS

IDEA PRESENTATION

It is the contest where there will be online games like Free-Fire.

Registration fees:


Team Size: Individual participant


Event Details:


Round1:
Abstract Submission: Participants must Submit Abstract of their ideas / innovations while time of registration. Abstract should contain problem statement info and basic short overview about your idea and innovation in 300-400 words. Abstract should be in PDF or MS Word. Based on Idea, Uniqueness, Creative solution, and Feasibility your idea will be selected for round 2.
Round 2:
Detailed PPT Submission: Shortlisted Participants from round 1 will get a mail of selection to round 2 which is PPT Submission Round of Their Ideas. In these round participants must submit Detailed PPT of their ideas / Innovations / Solution by covering all the Slide Titles Mentioned below:
Team Name, Title of Solution & College Name.

Problem statement
Concept of Idea Features of your Solution
Objective of Idea / Solution
Methodology Proposed
Target Beneficiary (Target Users of your idea)
Novelty of Solution
Proof Of concept
Video / Image (If available)
Output Result of Your Project
Any Start-up Initiated (If Yes then ADD otherwise SKIP)
End Thank You Slide.
Idea should be unique and original Work of participant.


Copied Solution will be not entertained, and Team will be disqualified if found any. After submission our team will review your ideas and then great and unique ideas will be shortlisted for Final round 3 which is final presentation round.


Round 3:

Finals (Presentation virtually to judges): Teams will receive mails of selection into finals. Here you must present your idea / solution to judges (Jury Panel) live on Google Meet. Each team will be allotted 5 minutes for presentation and Question and Answers from Judges related to your presented idea and solution.


The winner will be awarded with cash prizes.
