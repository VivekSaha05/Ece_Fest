GULLY CRICKET

Registration fees:


Team Size: Individual participant


Event Details:


Pitch-catch is considered out if you catch the ball in one hand.
When the fielding team member catches the ball after one bounce, but in one hand, the player who hit the ball is out!
First ball = Trial ball
The first ball that every batsman will attempt will always be a trial ball just so they can warm up their batting skills and get used to the bowling.
If the ball hits the neighbour’s house, window, wall or car, the batsman is out!
If the batsman hits the ball directly into the wall of the neighbour’s house, widow or the car, they will have to bear the expenses that follow, and they are out!
The winning team will always get to bat first in the next match!
The team that wins this match will automatically bat for the next match, because who doesn’t love to bat.
The batsman should always leave some space for the wicket to be seen because there is no concept of LBW!
The batsman should not cover the whole wicket while batting cause there is no LBW rule. And if you don’t, the bowler will throw a fit.
If the batsman thinks the ball is too fast, the bowler should re-bowl the ball!
If the batsman feels the ball was too fast, it is too fast. Oh, and that ball will not be counted because clearly, it’s unfair.
If the ball goes far out of reach or is lost, the batsman is held responsible.
When the batsman hits the ball so far that it’s lost, he will have to replace the ball.
The umpire is chosen from the batting team.
The umpire will be a player from the batting team because he’ll be sitting on the sidelines. When it’s his turn to bat, someone else will replace him!
The last batsman will play without a non-striker batsman.
The last man gets to bat without assistance from the other non-striker batsman. Because hey… we can make up their own rules.
The team with the extra player will have to lend him to play fielding for the other team!
The extra player in the team, usually the worst player will be asked to play for both teams. Obviously because then the powers of both the team will be equal.

STREET FOOTBALL

Registration fees:


Team Size: Group of 11 members in which 1 Goalkeeper and 10 outfield field players.


Event Details:


Teams will be told which way they are kicking at start of game.
Teams cannot score directly from a kick off.
There is no offside
All free kicks and corners are direct, and opposition must be 3 metres away.
Goal kicks to be taken anywhere inside penalty area. Opposition must be 3 metres away and ball must leave penalty area for play to recommence.
Substitutions can be made at any time from the halfway line. Substituted player must leave the pitch before replacement goes on.
Compulsory line up and handshake to finish.
Games will be 10 minutes each way in duration with a 2-minute half time
There is a 6-minute gap between each game. Teams must be ready to take the pitch as soon as the previous match has concluded.
Teams to have a matching kit in similar colours with goalkeeper having a distinctly different coloured shirt.
Correct equipment i.e., long socks, shin pads, football is a compulsory requirement.
The winner will be awarded with cash prizes.
