TREASURE HUNT

Registration fees:


Team Size: Each team can contain a maximum of 4 participants. Students from different department can form a single team.


Event Details:


Round 1:

Each team can contain a maximum of 4 participants. Students from different department can form a single team.

Round 2:

Dumb charades. It is a word Guessing game where your group will be given the title of a movie, 1 person should give the gestures and the other 3 should guess the title.

Round 3:

Finding Hints. In this your group must find the hidden prize by following special clues which have been left in different places.

The team which finally crack the 5th level treasure will be the winner.

The winner team will be rewarded with the cash prize.
