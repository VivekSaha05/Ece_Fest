MUSICAL CHAIR

Registration fees: Rs.10


Team Size: Individual participant


Event Details:


In this a music will be played and the players must walk around the chairs arranged in a circle. When the music stops, everyone must immediately sit in a chair. The one person left standing will be out. One chair will be removed, and it will be continued until only one person is left.
The one person left after completion of all the rounds, will be the winner.
The winner will be awarded with cash prizes.
